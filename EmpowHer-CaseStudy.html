EmpowHer Initiatives

A mobile networking experience designed to help women connect, find mentorship, and feel supported in male-dominated industries.

UX Research UI Design Mobile App Figma

Overview

EmpowHer is a networking platform built specifically for women in the industry — especially those in STEM — who often experience underrepresentation and isolation in the workforce.

The goal was to create a space where early-career professionals and experienced mentors could meaningfully connect, exchange knowledge, and grow together.

The Problem

Limited Discoverability

Users struggled to find professionals outside their industry. The algorithm limited cross-field exploration.

Static Explore Page

The Explore page felt passive and unengaging, reducing opportunities for connection.

Unclear Notifications

Users were unsure how they would know if they missed mentorship requests or connection invites.

What Worked Well

Intuitive Onboarding

Profile creation was simple and empowering. Users could clearly present their experience without feeling overwhelmed.

Mentorship Experience

The ability to connect with experienced professionals was the most valuable feature and reinforced the app’s purpose.

Clean Visual Design

Familiar interaction patterns and clear hierarchy made navigation seamless.

Final Solutions

🔎 Advanced Search & Filters

Enabled users to discover mentors and professionals across industries, empowering cross-field networking.

❤️ Interactive Engagement

Added Like/Love functionality to create dynamic interaction and encourage connection pathways.

🔔 Centralized Notifications Hub

Introduced a dedicated notifications page so users never miss posts, mentorship requests, or connection invites.
