ABOUT ME

My journey into tech began when I started my Software Engineering degree. I've always been curious about how things work, and that natural curiosity led me to explore the world of programming and design. University was a challenge at first, I was overwhelmed at times, but I quickly discovered how rewarding it could be. The harder it got, the more I wanted to push through. That sense of
