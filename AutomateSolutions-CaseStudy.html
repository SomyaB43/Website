Automate Solutions — Website Redesign

Redesigning the Automate Solutions website to improve usability, structure, and overall professionalism.

UX Design Website Redesign Information Architecture

Overview

Automate Solutions is a company specializing in developing software for mobile and desktop devices. The goal of this redesign was to improve usability, navigation clarity, and overall professionalism.

The original website made it difficult for users to quickly understand what the company offered and how to get in touch. This project focused on creating a clearer structure, stronger hierarchy, and a smoother user experience.

The Goal

  • Improve site navigation
  • Make services easier to understand
  • Create a stronger visual hierarchy
  • Add a clear contact pathway
  • Modernize the overall design

The Problem

  • Navigation was unclear and hard to follow
  • Services were not clearly categorized
  • The design felt basic and lacked structure
  • Animations felt unnatural and outdated
  • No strong call-to-action or easy contact flow

UX Approach

To improve the experience, the focus was on reducing cognitive load and making the website easier to scan and navigate.

  • Clear top-level navigation
  • Logical content grouping
  • Structured service categories
  • Simplified and natural animations
  • A dedicated contact page

Final Solutions

Improved Navigation Bar

A clear navigation bar was introduced separating About, Services, Work, and Contact. This allows users to instantly understand the site structure and move between pages easily.

Dedicated About Page

A structured About page was added to explain the company’s mission, build credibility, and guide users toward the Work page.

Clear Service Breakdown

Services were reorganized and categorized based on device type and functionality. Each section clearly explains what the company does, what devices are supported, and how it benefits the customer.

Contact Page With Direct Messaging

A dedicated contact page with a message form was created to provide a simple and direct way for users to reach the business owner, creating a clear conversion path.
