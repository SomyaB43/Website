Automate Solutions: Website Redesign

Improving usability, navigation, and overall professionalism for Automate Solutions.

UX Design Website Redesign Information Architecture

Overview

Automate Solutions specializes in software for mobile and desktop devices. The redesign focused on improving usability, navigation clarity, and professionalism.

The original site made it difficult for users to understand the services and get in touch. This project created clearer structure, stronger hierarchy, and smoother experience.

The Goal

  • Improve site navigation
  • Make services easier to understand
  • Create a stronger visual hierarchy
  • Add a clear contact pathway
  • Modernize the overall design

The Problem

  • Navigation was unclear and hard to follow
  • Services were not clearly categorized
  • The design felt basic and lacked structure
  • Animations felt unnatural and outdated
  • No clear call-to-action or contact flow

UX Approach

Focused on reducing cognitive load and making the site easier to scan and navigate.

  • Clear top-level navigation
  • Logical content grouping
  • Structured service categories
  • Simplified, natural animations
  • Dedicated contact page

Final Solutions

Improved Navigation Bar

Clear top-level nav separating About, Services, Work, and Contact. Users can understand the site structure and move easily between pages.

Dedicated About Page

Explains the company mission, builds credibility, and guides users to the Work page.

Clear Service Breakdown

Services organized by device type and functionality, clearly showing what the company does, devices supported, and benefits.

Contact Page With Messaging

A simple, direct contact form enables users to reach the business owner and provides a clear conversion path.
